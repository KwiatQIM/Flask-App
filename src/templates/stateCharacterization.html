Term Imbalance:

Defined as (|b/a|)2 in the equation ψ = a|HH> + b|VV>, assuming b ≤ a.

Decoherence:

The fraction of the maximum off-diagonal elements in the state's density matrix

Background:

The proportion of the background density that is measured (background density is the 4x4 identity matrix for a 2 qubit state)
