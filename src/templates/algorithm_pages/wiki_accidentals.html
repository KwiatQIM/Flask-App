Variables

  • Ti : The times in nano-seconds of measurement i given as an input. Default is 1.
  • Sijk : For measurement i and detector pair j, Sijk is the singles count on the kth dector in the pair. For example in the first detector pair 1-2, the Sij1 detector is the singles count on detector 1. Default of the singles count is 0.
  • Wk : The coincidence window duration for the jth detector pair given as an input. Default is 0.
  • Ek : The relative efficiency on the jth detector pair. Default is 1.

More details on the equation can be found in the Photonic State Tomography paper by Joe Altepeter. The accidental counts are calculated in the filter_data() function.
